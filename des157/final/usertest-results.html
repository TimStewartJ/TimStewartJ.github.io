User Test Results

I found this user test to be incredibly helpful. When making anything that ultimately goes into a user's hands, it's too easy to get caught up in one's own design. Seeing how other people interacted with and used my site was eye-opening.

One thing I noticed right off the bat was users' confusion with the phrase to try hovering over the numbers. This is because the numbers are not initially visible. The user has to first take off the cover. This makes me consider the text I have under the title and potentially switching the default to be covers off instead.

Users also tended to try to click the remove cover button on the second image, even though the button was clearly disabled. I'll have to experiment with other methods of making it more obvious that there is no cover to take off for that particular perspective.

Some other smaller comments I received were mentions of smaller improvments that could be made. One user said that they didn't even see the text under the title, even though it is a relatively important instruction. They said the large image draws the eyes from it. I could perhaps try using an overlay upon initial loading of the site, serving as both a welcome and a loading screen for all of the images.
